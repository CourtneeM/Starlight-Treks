Choose From Three Night Hiking Experiences

Junior Explorer Trail

Are you new to hiking or an experienced hiker looking for a less strenuous hike, while also experiencing an amazing view of the stars at the end? This hike may be the perfect choice for you! This is our shortest trail with no significant inclines or slopes.

Beginner Hikers

The Mountaineer Trail

More experienced hikers looking for a reward at the end of their journey will not be disappointed with this trail. This trail features a more advanced route compared to our beginner trail. The Mountaineer Trail is twice as long as our shortest trail.

Experienced Hikers

Starlight Sleepover Trail

Hiking enthusiasts who want to have a truly memorable experience will not forget this trail soon. The Starlight Sleepover Trail is the same route as The Mountaineer Trail, but includes sleeping at our campsite located at the end of the trail.
